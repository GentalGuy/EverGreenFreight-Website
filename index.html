Services

LTL

Less-than-truckload shipping transports freight that does not require a full truckload trailer.

FTL

A means of transportation in which merchandise is conveyed in a fully loaded truck trailer.

FLATBED/STEP-DECK TRAILERS

We offer skilled flatbed trucking service in the United States and Canada for any business.

REFRIGERATED (REEFER)

Our organization also provides freight that requires specialized, temperature-controlled settings for transit.

INTERMODAL

Evergreen also offers overseas intermodal shipping through ports in eastern and western California. Intermodal transportation involves moving goods from one mode of transport to another.

OVERSIZE SHIPMENTS

Our team will work with you to understand your shipping volume and size and patterns and then design a collaborate solution that best meets your specific shipping needs.

EDI Integration

Transfer data between systems using a standard set of formats. It increases the speed of business transactions and reduce cost associated with manual documents processing, such as mail delivery.

EXPEDITED

A coverage network that spans major cities all across the United States and Canada. Expedited shipping is a type of shipping service that delivers goods faster that regular shipping.
